# Healthy Tracker

Healthy Tracker supports a beginner building sustainable exercise and eating habits while interpreting body-composition changes as trends rather than daily judgments.

## Language

**Baseline**:
The starting health measurements and routine used to contextualize later trends.
_Avoid_: Starting score, target

**Workout Session**:
A planned period of machine strength work, with an optional Walk–run block.
_Avoid_: Training day, gym visit

**Exercise Log**:
The recorded Weight, Reps, RPE, and pain signal for one machine exercise within a Workout Session.
_Avoid_: Set record, performance score

**Meal Template**:
A reusable meal option with an estimated Portion, Calories, and Protein.
_Avoid_: Diet prescription, recipe

**Daily Nutrition Log**:
The Meal Templates and custom meals recorded for one calendar day.
_Avoid_: Diet score, meal plan result

**InBody Record**:
A dated body-composition measurement containing the five metrics used for long-term comparison.
_Avoid_: Health diagnosis, body score

**Adherence**:
The proportion of planned Workout Sessions and nutrition actions completed over a review period.
_Avoid_: Compliance, streak

**Progression**:
A deliberate increase in machine Weight after both working sets reach the top of the Rep range at manageable RPE.
_Avoid_: Personal record, level up

**Walk–run Stage**:
A two-week interval pattern that gradually lengthens jogging while keeping the total block short.
_Avoid_: Pace target, running test
